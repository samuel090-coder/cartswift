# Google Search Console Setup Instructions

## Step 1: Add Your Site to Google Search Console
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/)
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Choose "URL prefix" and enter: `https://cartswift.lovable.app`
4. Follow the verification process (HTML file upload or DNS verification)

## Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap
1. Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
2. Click "Add/Test Sitemap"
3. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
4. Click "Submit"

## Step 3: Request Indexing
1. Go to "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter your homepage URL: `https://cartswift.lovable.app`
3. Click "Request Indexing" to get your site crawled faster
